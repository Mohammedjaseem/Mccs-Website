Department of Computer Science

Department Overview

The Department of Computer Science was established in 2014. The department started its functioning with the commencement of the UG course BCA (Bachelor of Computer Application). The main aim of the department is to provide quality education to aspiring graduates. The course is specifically designed three year programme which aims to develop required professional skills and problem solving ability.
