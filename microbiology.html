Department of Microbiology

Department Overview

Welcome to the Department of Microbiology, established in 2014, where we offer a dynamic three-year B.Sc. program to 36 students. Our well-equipped laboratory facilitates hands-on learning and innovation. Our experienced faculty supports academic, extracurricular, and research activities. Our alumni excel in prestigious institutions and prominent positions globally. Our program cultivates scientific, analytical, and problem-solving skills, covering microorganism characteristics, applications in drug production, pollution control, and more.

We provide courses in English, biology, research methodology, and intellectual property rights. Students engage in biochemistry, biostatistics, computer science, and compulsory social service activities. Our department conducts research on economically important microbes and clinical pathogens. Graduates emerge with robust microbiological knowledge, research skills, and career opportunities in food industries, pharmaceuticals, medical research, agriculture, or higher education. Join us in the world of Microbiology at MCCS, where knowledge meets opportunity, and discovery thrives.

Why Microbiology Department is Special?

  • Vision

    To build a dynamic learning environment based on fundamental scientific concepts, where novel Microbiological knowledge is generated and shared, and where they can find joy and fulfilment throughout educational and recreational activities.

  • Mission

    The establishment of scholarly processes that promote scientific advancement through a strategic approach and an exclusive national vision for science and technology, to educate scholar, to introduce a methodology designed to meet the various needs of society to serve the community.

Assistant Professor & HOD

An accomplished microbiologist, earned a Ph.D. in Microbiology from the University of Calicut in 2022.
