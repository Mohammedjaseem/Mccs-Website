    Management Admission Procedure

    Follow these simple steps to secure your seat at MCCS

    1

    Application Submission

    Complete the online application form available on our website or collect the form from the admission office. Ensure all details are accurately filled.

    2

    Document Verification

    Submit required documents (originals + photocopies) for verification. Our admission team will review your eligibility based on academic records.

    3

    Rank List Publication

    The rank list will be released based on the candidates performance in the selection process.

    4

    Admission Offer

    Successful candidates will receive provisional admission offers via email/SMS within 7 working days of assessment.

    5

    Payment (Gov. fees only)

    Secure your admission by paying the first installment of fees within the stipulated time frame through available payment methods.

    6

    Registration Complete

    After fee payment, complete the enrollment formalities and collect your admission kit containing important college information.

    Eligibility Criteria

    Academic requirements for our various programs

    Undergraduate Programs

    • Completion of 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board
    • Minimum aggregate marks required vary by program (45-60%)
    • Science programs require PCM/PCB in 10+2
    • Commerce programs require Commerce/Mathematics in 10+2
    • BA English requires English as a subject in 10+2

    Special Categories

    • Sports quota: State/national level achievements required
    • Management quota: Limited seats available
    • SC/ST/OBC: Relaxation as per government norms

    Documents Required

    Checklist of documents needed for admission

    • Completed application form with passport-size photographs
    • Original mark sheets and certificates of qualifying examinations
    • Transfer Certificate from previous institution
    • Conduct Certificate from previous institution
    • Migration Certificate (for students from other boards/universities)
    • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
    • Aadhaar Card copy
    • Sports achievement certificates (for sports quota)
    • Any other relevant certificates
