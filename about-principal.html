    Principal's Desk

    Leadership, Vision, and Commitment to Excellence in Education

    Prasad K

    Principal, Malabar College of Commerce and Science

    PhD in Commerce, MBA, M.Com, B.Ed

    With over 25 years of experience in academia and educational administration, Prasad K has been at the helm of Malabar College of Commerce and Science since 2021, guiding the institution towards academic excellence and innovation.

    Under his leadership, MCCS has achieved significant milestones including NAAC accreditation, infrastructure development, and establishment of industry partnerships that enhance student employability.

    Principal's Message

    Dear Students, Parents, and Well-wishers,

    It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to Malabar College of Commerce and Science, an institution committed to nurturing young minds and preparing them for the challenges of the modern world.

    At MCCS, we believe that education is not just about acquiring knowledge but about developing the whole person. Our holistic approach combines rigorous academics with co-curricular activities, value education, and skill development to ensure our students emerge as confident, competent, and compassionate individuals.

    In today's rapidly changing world, we emphasize innovation, critical thinking, and adaptability. Our faculty members are dedicated to creating a stimulating learning environment that encourages curiosity and independent thought. We take pride in our state-of-the-art facilities that support both theoretical learning and practical application.

    To our students, I say: This is your time to explore, to question, to grow. Make the most of the opportunities available to you. Remember that true success comes not just from what you achieve, but from the person you become in the process.

    To parents and guardians, I assure you that we consider it our privilege to partner with you in shaping your ward's future. Our doors are always open for dialogue and collaboration.

    I invite you to explore our website to learn more about our programs, facilities, and the vibrant life at MCCS. Better still, visit our campus to experience firsthand the MCCS difference.

    Prasad K
    Principal

    Professional Achievements

    Key milestones in Dr. Prasad K.'s distinguished career

    Academic Leadership

    Successfully led MCCS through NAAC accreditation process with 'B+' grade in 2018, establishing quality for the institution.

    Research Contributions

    Published over 25 research papers in reputed journals and authored 3 books on commerce education and management practices.

    Curriculum Innovation

    Pioneered the introduction of skill-based certificate programs alongside degree courses to enhance student employability.

    International Collaboration

    Established academic partnerships with 3 international universities.

    Community Engagement

    Initiated 'MCCS Outreach' program connecting college resources with local community development projects.

    Awards & Recognition

    Recipient of 'Best Principal Award' by Kerala Commerce Association in 2020 for outstanding contribution to commerce education.
