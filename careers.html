    Application Process

    How to join the MCCS team

    1

    Submit Application

    Complete the online application form and upload your CV, cover letter, and supporting documents.

    2

    Review Process

    Our HR team will review your application and contact shortlisted candidates within 2 weeks.

    3

    Interview

    Selected candidates will be invited for an interview with the department head and selection committee.

    4

    Demo Class (for faculty)

    Teaching candidates will be asked to conduct a demo class on a relevant topic.

    5

    Reference Check

    We will contact your professional references as part of the evaluation process.

    6

    Offer Letter

    Successful candidates will receive an offer letter with details of compensation and benefits.
