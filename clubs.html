    MCCS Clubs

    Young Innovators Programme (YIP)

    We take pride in our active participation in the Young Innovators Programme (YIP), an initiative by the Kerala Development and Innovation Strategic Council (K-DISC). YIP empowers our students to become innovators, addressing real-world challenges with creative solutions. This program not only recognizes our students' innovative spirit but also opens doors to opportunities that can transform ideas into reality.

    Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC)

    Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) at Malabar College of Commerce and Science was initiated in the year 2019 with a vision of bringing out successful entrepreneurs from the Institute.

    The aim of this initiative is to foster an entrepreneurial culture among young people and provide them with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to become future leaders in entrepreneurship. Objective of the cell is to accelerate the successful development of entrepreneurial companies through an array of business support resources and services. EDC plays a major role in molding student entrepreneurs.

    Program Committee

    The program committee conducted various programs. All the special days, events, and cultural activities are under the program committee.

    Vimukthi Club

    The Vimukthi Club in the college is constituted with the primary objective of promoting a drug free and healthy lifestyle among students. A vigilant committee helps raise awareness about the ill-effects of drug abuse through regular conduct of programmes which include expert talks, competitions, awareness drives etc. The activities of the Club include educating the students about the harmful effects of drugs, alcohol and other narcotic substances and reporting of any noticed use of drugs by the students to the members of the Club.

    Tourism Club

    The Tourism Club of our college is formed with an objective of creating awareness among the students about Tourism activities and to incorporate the student's strength and potential in the development of tourism in our State.

    Nature Club

    Nature club of MCCS was formed to educate, motivate and inspire students about environmental awareness and protection. The students should understand the value of nature and different environmental issues. Preservation of nature is the utmost aim of each and every individual. A pollution free environment is essential for all living beings. Nature club has about 48 students from all the departments. They strive hard to develop a love of nature, protection of the environment and campus. The theme for Environmental day 2023 was 'BEAT PLASTIC POLLUTION'- highlighting people's actions toward plastic pollution reduction.

    Under the leadership of the National Service Scheme (NSS), an impactful program titled "Waste to Best" took place. As part of this initiative, waste baskets were creatively crafted from paint buckets. In line with this, the department initiated the creation of a mini vermicompost unit. The College Union presented a beautiful money plant to every class, and the class representatives eagerly received this thoughtful gift, and they also donated a waste basket to the campus adding another delightful attraction to the event.

    Additionally, a Quiz competition was held. Also celebrated Farmers Day in association with NSS. Our memorable visit to Chairman Dr. CP Alibava Haji's farm was made possible by the dedication of our NSS volunteers and Nature Club members. Together, we celebrated Farmers' Day and immersed ourselves in the world of Rambutan, Mangosteen, Cherry, Dragon Fruit, and more. Connecting with nature and learning about cultivation was an enriching experience for all.

    Celluloid Film & Photography Club

    Photography is a wonderful hobby and has proved to be very popular among the students. This club focuses on taking and collecting fine photographic shots and participating in photographic competitions. Students can also learn many of the technicalities involved in digital as well as film based photography.

    The aim of the club is to promote photographic interest among students and provide a place for anyone with an interest in photography to meet and exchange ideas and share experiences. The club organizes various lectures, demonstrations, and competitions. It is our practice to welcome everyone with an interest in photography, regardless of their experience.

    Anti-Ragging Harassment Club

    The role of Anti-Ragging Squad will be to keep a vigil and stop the incidences of Ragging, if any, happening / reported in the places of Student aggregation including, Classrooms, Canteens, Buses, Grounds, Hostels etc.

    Ragging is a punishable offense and the victim is guarded by the rules and regulations of the government. A single or a group of students who are introduced in ragging can be handed over to the police and can be sent out of the college, banned admission in other educational institutions.
