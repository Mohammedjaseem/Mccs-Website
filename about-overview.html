    About Malabar College of Commerce and Science

    Empowering students through quality education, innovation, and holistic development since our inception.

    Our College Overview

    Malabar College of Commerce and Science (MCCS) is a premier educational institution located in Edappal, Malappuram, Kerala. Established with a vision to provide quality education in commerce and science streams, MCCS has grown to become a center of academic excellence in the region.

    Our college offers a diverse range of undergraduate programs designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a vibrant campus life, we are committed to nurturing the next generation of professionals and leaders.

    • Quality education since inception
    • Student-centered learning approach
    • Focus on holistic development
    • Strong industry-academia collaboration

    Our History & Milestones

    Journey through our significant achievements and growth over the years

    2005

    Establishment

    Malabar College of Commerce and Science was founded with a vision to provide quality higher education in the region.

    2010

    Expansion

    Introduced new programs in science and technology, expanding our academic offerings to meet industry demands.

    2015

    Infrastructure Upgrade

    Completed major infrastructure development including modern laboratories and a digital library.

    2018

    NAAC Accreditation

    Achieved NAAC accreditation with 'B+' grade, recognizing our commitment to quality education.

    2020

    Digital Transformation

    Implemented comprehensive digital learning platforms to enhance the teaching-learning experience.

    2023

    New Horizons

    Launched innovative programs and strengthened industry partnerships for better student outcomes.
