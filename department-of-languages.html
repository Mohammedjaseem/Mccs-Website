    Department of Languages

    Department Overview

    The Department of Languages at MCCS was established in 2020 and offers a three-year B.Sc. program with a focus on English, Malayalam, Hindi, and Arabic, along with interdisciplinary courses in research, cultural studies, and applied linguistics. Supported by a dedicated faculty and a modern language lab, the program equips students with strong communication, analytical, and research skills.

    Graduates gain expertise for careers in translation, content writing, education, media, and research, while certificate courses enhance employability. The department also promotes holistic development through seminars, workshops, and community engagement initiatives.
