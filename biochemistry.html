    Department of Biochemistry

    Department Overview

    The Department of Biochemistry at MCCS was established in 2020. It offers a three-year B.Sc. program with a total intake capacity of 24 students. The department has a well-equipped laboratory for student use and a dedicated faculty. The B.Sc. program covers general and clinical aspects of Biochemistry, along with courses in English, second languages (Malayalam, Hindi, Arabic), and general subjects like biological diversity, natural resource management, research methodology, and intellectual property rights. Complementary courses include Microbiology and Chemistry, and students also engage in compulsory social service activities. Institutional visits, seminars, workshops, and career orientation programs are part of the curriculum.

    Graduates of the UG Biochemistry program at MCCS gain knowledge in Biochemistry, research skills, analytical abilities, and the capacity to prepare project reports. They also acquire a basic understanding of Microbiology, Chemistry, languages, and general courses. This prepares them for higher education or careers in fields like food industries, pharmaceuticals, medical, clinical research, or agriculture. In addition to the university curriculum, the college offers certificate programs to enhance students' job skills and make them more job-oriented.

    Why Biochemistry Department is Special?

    Biochemistry offers opportunities in medical, para-medical fields, and food industries, as well as research. Graduates can work in research labs, product development, healthcare, forensics, and various other fields. Biochemistry is a flexible discipline with endless career possibilities. Job Oppurtunities include:

    Bio-technologist,   Research Scientist,   Clinical Scientist,   Microbiologist,   Chemist,   Biomedical Scientist, Pharmacologist,   Laboratory Technicians

      Producing quality students trained in the latest tools and technologies and striving to make university a world leader in the area of biochemistry. To achieve academic excellence in biochemistry of imparting in-depth knowledge to the students, facilitating research activities and cater to the ever changing industrial demand & societal needs. To be a centre of excellence in the discipline of biochemistry.

    • Mission

      To provide quality education to the students through state of art and education in biochemistry. To provide a learning environment that helps the students to enhance problem solving skills, be successful in their professionalives and to prepare students to be lifelong learner by offering solid theoretical & practical foundation in various discipline of biochemistry and educating them about their professional and ethical responsibilities. To provide exposure to students to the latest tools and technologies in the area of biochemistry. To provide research based projects activities in the emerging area of biochemical technology. To develop the man resource with sound knowledge theory & practical in the disciple of biochemistry and the ability to apply knowledge to the benefit of the society at large.

    CANCER AWARENESS SESSION

    The Dept. Of Biochemistry, conducted a cancer awareness class led by Dr. Phinse M Philip, Dept. of Community Oncology, Malabar Cancer Centre, Kannur. We feel very happy to be able to conduct such a wonderful program in the beginning of 2023. The session was marvelous. The students were able to understand cancer a little bit better. All the doubts students had about cancer were cleared through an interactive session. Teachers and students also received practicals to detect breast cancer. A very positive feedback was received from the students through this session. So heartfelt thanks to the Principal and Academic Director for giving permission and providing all the support for this program. Also thankful to all colleagues who supported this program.

    IV VISIT

    On January 13, 2023, the Department of Biochemistry embarked on a journey to the MS Swaminathan Research Foundation in Wayanad. Departing from Edappal at 3 AM, we reached the institution at 9 AM. During our visit, we attended a captivating class on plant research, exploring the realms of biodiversity and the medicinal potential of various plant species. Ms. Dhanya, a scientist, and Mr. Salim, a subject expert, enriched our understanding of these topics. We extend our heartfelt gratitude to the institution's staff for this enlightening experience. It marked a memorable start to 2023, and students provided positive feedback. After immersing ourselves in Wayanad's natural beauty, we returned safely to Edappal at 11:30 PM on January 14, 2023, with a newfound appreciation for plant research and biodiversity.

    IGNITE THE SPARK

    An induction program was organized for the 2023-2026 batch on 17/8/23, attended by students from Biochemistry, Microbiology, and Food Technology departments. Mr. Nishad Pattayil, an international corporate trainer, led a session titled "IGNITE THE SPARK" with the Principal, Vice Principal, Department Heads, and faculty in attendance. The program aimed to introduce students to college culture, communicate the code of conduct, prepare them for career goals, and encourage skill development. Mr. Pattayil effectively conveyed the program's objectives to the students in a concise and engaging manner.
