Department of Food Technology

Department Overview

Welcome to the Department of Food Technology at Malabar College of Commerce and Science. Established in September 2022, our BSc Food Technology programme is a dynamic three-year undergraduate course that delves into the preservation, processing, and manufacturing of food.

This course opens doors to a world of opportunities for food technology enthusiasts, offering the potential for careers in research, as food technologists, quality assurance analysts, or packaging managers in leading companies.

Why Food Technology Department is Special?

Our BSc Food Technology program boasts a capacity of 24 seats, with core courses focused on food technology and complementary courses in physics and chemistry. Elective courses include dairy technology, and students also engage in audit courses such as environmental studies and disaster management.

  • Vision

    Our vision is to provide students with a high-quality education in food technology, fostering fundamental skill development and practical knowledge. We prepare our students for diverse roles in the global food industry and advanced studies.

  • Mission

    Our mission is to conduct comprehensive research in food science and technology, contributing to our nation's food safety and security. We embrace transdisciplinary approaches to promote safe, sustainable, and healthy food production through innovative research, teaching, and industrial outreach.
