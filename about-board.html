    Board of Directors

    Leadership guiding the vision and governance of Malabar College of Commerce and Science

    Our Governing Body

    The Board of Directors at Malabar College of Commerce and Science provides strategic leadership and oversight to ensure the institution fulfills its mission of delivering exceptional education.

    Comprising distinguished professionals from academia, industry, and community leadership, the Board establishes policies, approves strategic plans, and monitors institutional performance.

    • Sets the strategic direction for the college
    • Ensures financial sustainability
    • Upholds academic standards
    • Represents community interests

    Board Responsibilities

    Key functions performed by our Board of Directors

    Strategic Planning

    Establishing the long-term vision and strategic direction for the college's growth and development.

    Financial Oversight

    Approving budgets, monitoring financial performance, and ensuring proper resource allocation.

    Quality Assurance

    Setting policies to maintain and enhance academic standards and institutional quality.

    Leadership Appointment

    Selecting and evaluating the performance of the Principal and senior administrators.

    Community Engagement

    Building relationships with stakeholders and representing community interests.

    Policy Governance

    Establishing policies that guide institutional operations and ensure compliance.
