Department of Psychology

Department Overview

Welcome to our Psychology Department, established in 2022. We offer a transformative three-year B.Sc. program in Psychology for 24 students. Our well-equipped lab is a canvas for exploration. Our faculty, experts in the field, engage students in projects and extracurricular activities. The B.Sc. Psychology program covers foundational themes, individual differences, and courses in Statistics and Human Physiology. Students explore Abnormal Psychology, Psychological Counselling, Social Psychology, and Health Psychology. Practical courses provide hands-on experience, and small projects hone research and statistical skills.

We promote mental health awareness through debates, discussions, and awareness programs. We plan to establish a counseling unit in the near future. Graduates gain profound knowledge of psychology, analytical skills, and report preparation abilities. They are prepared for careers in counseling, clinical psychology, health psychology, and more. Join us in the world of psychology at MCCS, where minds are nurtured, perspectives are broadened, and a compassionate future unfolds. Graduates contribute significantly to the field of psychology.

Why Psychology Department is Special?

Our Psychology Department is a hub of academic excellence and personal growth. Our dedicated, experienced teachers provide top-tier psychology education. Our exceptional collection of psychology books fuels in-depth exploration. Beyond academics, we create an environment for holistic development, offering counseling services for students facing challenges. MCCS fosters a passion for psychology, cultivating critical thinking and career readiness. Explore the captivating world of psychology with us on this transformative journey.
