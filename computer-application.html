    Department of BCA

    Department Overview

    The Department of BCA was established in 2014. The department started its functioning with the commencement of the UG course BCA (Bachelor of Computer Application). The main aim of the department is to provide quality education to aspiring graduates. The course is specifically designed three year programme which aims to develop required professional skills and problem solving ability.

    Why BCA Department is Special?

    • Vision

      After acquiring degree, there are further educational opportunities for higher studies like MSc, MCA, MBA, etc. at any universities. They will also get professional jobs in IT industries after completing this course.

    • Mission

      The department is a unique hub which provides sufficient support and back up for practical knowledge and advanced learning in the field of IT. Apart from the curriculum offered by the University, the college offers the add-on courses for the benefits of the students. The college aims at imparting crucial job skills to the students, through various certificate programs, thus enabling them to acquire an additional certificate along with their degree certificate, making the students job oriented that’s the motive of the college.
