    General Eligibility Criteria

    For Undergraduate Programmes:

    • Pass in Higher Secondary Examination (10+2) or equivalent
    • Science stream required for BSc programmes (except Psychology)
    • Minimum 50% aggregate marks for general category (45% for reserved categories)
    • Mathematics compulsory for BSc Computer Science applicants

    Admission Process

    Steps to join MCCS academic programmes

    1

    Application Submission

    Complete the online application form available on our website or collect the form from the college office. Submit before the deadline with required documents.

    2

    Document Verification

    Bring original certificates and mark sheets for verification. This includes 10th, 12th, and degree certificates (for PG applicants).

    3

    Entrance Test (if applicable)

    Some programmes may require an entrance examination. Details will be communicated after application submission.

    4

    Merit List Publication

    Merit lists will be published on the college notice board and website based on academic performance and entrance test scores.

    5

    Fee Payment

    Selected candidates must pay the admission fee within the stipulated time to confirm their seat.

    6

    Orientation Programme

    Attend the mandatory orientation programme before classes begin to familiarize yourself with college facilities and academic policies.
