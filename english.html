Department of English

Department Overview

The MCCS English Department offers an enriching educational experience in English language and literature. Our diverse programs cater to students' interests and goals, including a Bachelor of Arts in English Language and Literature, which builds strong foundations in analysis and communication. Our faculty, highly qualified and passionate, fosters a stimulating learning environment. They support students in and out of the classroom. We host events like guest lectures, literary festivals, and writing competitions, encouraging engagement and talent showcase.

The English Department organizes a variety of events and activities throughout the year to foster a sense of community and engagement among students, faculty, and alumni. These include guest lectures by renowned scholars and writers, literary festivals, poetry readings, and writing competitions. Such events provide platforms for networking, showcasing talent, and exploring the richness of the English language and literature.

Why English Department is Special?

An education in English Language and Literature opens up a wide range of career opportunities . Graduates from our department have pursued successful careers in fields such as publishing, journalism, education, marketing, public relations, writing, translation and more. The analytical and communication skills developed through our programmes equip students with the versatility and adaptability required in today's dynamic job market.


  • Vision

    To be a leading center of excellence in English studies, fostering literary appreciation, critical thinking, and language proficiency for a globally connected world.

  • Mission

    Our mission is to foster a deep appreciation for literature and language while equipping students with essential skills for a globalized world. Through academic excellence and cultural engagement, we empower individuals to think critically and communicate effectively.

About HOD

Sefiya M is an esteemed educator with a career spanning since 2008. Beginning as a Guest Lecturer in Government Higher Secondary Schools and later as an Academic Coordinator at Ansar English School and HSSTin Ideal English Higher Secondary School.she has amassed valuable teaching experience. Currently serving as the Head of the English Department at Malabar College of Commerce and Science, Manoor, a role she's held since 2021, she continues to excel in the field.

Beyond academia, Sefiya is a proficient Life Skills Trainer, conducting sessions on soft skills, parenting, and offering workshops for English teachers. She is also the founder of Talents Planet Skill Development Centre, providing expertise in Communicative English Training and Public Speaking.
